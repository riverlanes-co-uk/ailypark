Our history

Our history dates back to 1863 when First National Bank of Cincinnati opened for business. As regional banks proliferated across the country in the years that followed, a number of other predecessors were born. After a series of mergers at the turn of the 21st century, we formally took on the ISBANK Bank name and established our headquarters in Minnesota. Today, ISBANK Bank is the fifth-largest bank in the country, with more than 70,000 employees and $495 billion in assets as of December 31, 2019.
